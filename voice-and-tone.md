## Email 02 · Settlement Letter 1 — title insurance

**When:** four weeks before settlement.
**Voice rule:** Plain English, but more formal than Email 01 — this is a reference document. Avoid jargon.
**Subject:** `Settlement Letter 1 — your home, four weeks out`

**Body:**
```
Hi Regina,

Settlement is four weeks away. This is the first of two formal letters from me. I've also sent a copy to Tom (your lawyer).

What's happening:
- We're finishing your home this week and next.
- Council will sign off the title (the CCC) in late April.
- I'll send Settlement Letter 2 in three weeks with the final settlement statement.

What you need to know about title insurance:
Your sale agreement requires title insurance. We've arranged it through [insurer name]. The certificate is attached, and it's also in your portal under Documents → Insurance.

What you need to do:
- Read this letter and keep it on file.
- Tom will let you know when funds need to be in your trust account. It's usually 2 working days before settlement.
- Forward this to anyone else (e.g. your accountant) if useful.

Anything unclear, just reply or call me.

Damien
```
