## Document categories — when to use each

- **Settlement** — settlement letters, settlement statements, final invoice
- **Legal** — sale agreement, council CCC, title docs, encumbrances
- **Construction** — plans, elevations, schedule of materials, warranties
- **Insurance** — title insurance certificate, builder's insurance proof
- **Other** — anything else (parking allocation, body corp docs, etc.)
